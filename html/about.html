Hi, I'm Elena.

I'm a designer based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. I enjoy designing systems, working with typography, doodling in the margins, and constantly asking "why".

These days, I'm pushing design toward its remote future at Superside. Most recently, I helped research and build a social marketplace for Peddler and led a product team to build a business intelligence product for Tradefox. Before that, I led the team that launched flight search at Booking.com. I've also designed for Firmhouse, Eatapp, and Amberscript.

Want to work together? I'm always ready to hear about a new complexity that needs solving. Let's talk.
