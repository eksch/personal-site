Hi, I'm Elena.

I'm a designer based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. I enjoy designing systems, working with typography, doodling in the margins, and constantly asking "why".

I taught myself HTML and CSS when I was 13. Creating web pages was a hobby through university, where I studied graphic design, journalism, and art. (P.S. — The first full website I ever designed is still live!)

Since then, I've built digital products for several industries, including insurance, travel, e-commerce, and global trade; from mobile apps, to responsive websites, to data- and feature-heavy enterprise web applications.

I also create illustrations, calligraphy, and custom lettering for a variety of occasions.
